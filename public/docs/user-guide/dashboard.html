Dashboard

Your home screen after login — summary cards, shortcuts, and personal attendance.

Summary cards

Four coloured cards at the top provide at-a-glance metrics:

  • Active Employees (teal) — total active headcount. Click view all to open the employee list.
  • Departments (pink) — number of departments. Respects company/location permissions when enabled.
  • Today's Headcount (orange) — employees marked present today.
  • On Leave today (purple) — employees on fully approved leave for today's date.

Shortcuts

Four white shortcut tiles link directly to major admin modules:

  • Manage Employees → Employee Management
  • Payroll → Payroll module
  • Attendance → Attendance module
  • Leave Management → Leave Management module

Each tile opens in the same window. Shortcut visibility depends on your permissions.

My Attendance table

Below the shortcuts, the My [Month] [Year] Attendance section shows your personal clock-in/out records for the current month with columns:

  • # — row number
  • Date — attendance date
  • In time — clock-in time
  • Out Time — clock-out time
  • Status — present, absent, late, etc.

If no records exist yet, the table displays "No data available".

Top bar context

The dashboard header shows your selected Company and active Financial Year. Changing company context may update the summary card counts.

Permission-based content Cards, shortcuts, and widgets appear only when your role has the matching permissions.

How to return to the dashboard

Click Dashboard at the top of the left sidebar, or navigate to /dashboard.
