Vehicle Management

Register company vehicles and track assignment history to employees.

Vehicles

Vehicle Management → Vehicles

  • Register fleet vehicles with registration number, make, model, and status.
  • Mark vehicles as available, assigned, or out of service.
  • Edit vehicle details when fleet changes.

Assignment History

Vehicle Management → Assignment History

  1. Select a vehicle and assign it to an employee with a start date.
  2. Record return/end date when the assignment closes.
  3. View full assignment history per vehicle or per employee.

Assigned employees see their vehicle under Self Service → My Vehicle.
