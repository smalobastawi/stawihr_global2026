Leave Management

Configure leave policies, process applications, and generate leave reports.

Setup

Under Leave Management → Setup:

  • Manage Holidays — organization-specific holidays.
  • Public Holidays — national/public holiday calendar.
  • Weekly Holidays — regular non-working days.
  • Leave Types — annual, sick, maternity, compassionate, etc.
  • Leave Groups — policy bundles assigned to employees.

Operations

Menu itemPurpose
Rollover LeavesCarry forward unused balances to a new year
Apply Leave On BehalfHR submits leave for an employee
Pending ApprovalsAdmin view of all pending leave requests
Manual UploadsBulk import leave records
Leave AdjustmentsManually correct leave balances
Leave ScheduleOrganizational leave planning calendar
All ApplicationsSearch and filter every leave record

Reports

Under Leave Management → Reports:

  • Leave Report, Summary Report, My Leave Report
  • Leave Balances, On Leave Today
  • Full Org Report, Leave History, Leave Encashment
