Employee Management

Maintain employee records, organizational structure, contracts, and HR reports.

Manage employees

  1. Open Employee Management → Manage Employees.
  2. Click Add Employee and complete personal, job, and contact details.
  3. Assign department, section, designation, location, and supervisor.
  4. Upload documents and photo as required by your organization.
  5. Save and create a linked user account under Administration if the employee needs system access.

Organizational setup

ScreenPurpose

SectionsSub-units within departments

Location / RegionsPhysical work sites and regional groupings
Staff ContractsContract types and terms

Termination

Use Termination to process employee exits and Termination Checklist to ensure all offboarding steps are completed before deactivating accounts.

Employee reports

Under Employee Management → Reports:

  • Joiners Report — new hires in a date range.
  • Leavers Report — employees who exited.
  • Master Roll — complete employee listing.
  • Employee Turnover Report — turnover analysis.
Workforce charts Gender distribution and staff contract status charts are available on Analytics → Headcount Report, not on the Manage Employees list.

Company vehicles are managed separately — see Vehicle Management.
