Recruitment

Manage job requisitions, postings, and candidate pipelines.

Job Requisitions

Recruitment → Job Requisitions — request new headcount with department, role, and justification. Requisitions typically pass through an approval workflow before a job can be posted.

Job Post

Recruitment → Job Post — publish approved vacancies as internal, external, or both. Include job description, requirements, and closing date.

Job Candidates

Recruitment → Job Candidates

  1. Review applications and CVs submitted internally (ESS → Job Posts) or externally (public careers page).
  2. Update candidate status through your hiring stages (shortlisted, interviewed, offered, rejected).
  3. On hire, create the employee record under Employee Management.
