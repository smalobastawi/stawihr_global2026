Analytics

Visual HR insights and workforce metrics for management and HR teams.

HR Insights

Analytics → HR Insights

The analytics dashboard provides charts and KPIs that may include:

  • Headcount trends over time
  • Department and location breakdowns
  • Leave utilisation patterns
  • Turnover and joiners/leavers analysis
  • Attendance and overtime summaries

Available charts depend on your permissions and the data collected in other modules. Use filters for company, department, and date range where provided.
