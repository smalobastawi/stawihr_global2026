Analytics

Interactive HR dashboards with summary metrics, charts, and drill-down exploration across workforce, payroll, attendance, and more.

Analytics hub

Open Analytics → HR Insights to see the analytics hub — a card for each report category. Click a card to open that report’s dashboard with summary bubbles and charts.

Each chart includes an Explore link to view underlying data in a filterable table. Use year and other filters at the top of each report page where available.

Permissions You only see report categories your role is allowed to access. If a card is missing, ask your administrator to review Administration → Role Permissions.

Headcount Report

The Headcount Report (Analytics → HR Insights → Headcount Report) is the primary workforce overview. Summary bubbles at the top show totals such as active employees, new hires, terminations, and turnover rate.

Charts on this report include:

  • Department Distribution — headcount by department.
  • Gender Distribution — male/female/other breakdown (previously shown on Manage Employees; now lives here).
  • Staff Contracts — active vs expiring contracts (expiring = end date within three months).
  • Employee Hires and Employee Exits — trends over time.
  • Turnover Rate by Department and Retention Trends.

Click Explore on any chart to open a detailed data view with export options.

Tips for using analytics

  • Apply year and module-specific filters before comparing periods.
  • Use Explore on a chart when you need employee-level or transaction-level detail.
  • Cross-reference with module reports (e.g. Employee Management joiners/leavers reports) for formal HR reporting.
  • Charts reflect data entered in other modules — ensure employee records, payroll, attendance, and leave are up to date for accurate insights.
