Training

Manage training types, facilitators, sessions, and participation reports.

Setup screens

  • Training Type — categories such as compliance, technical, leadership.
  • Training Facilitator — register internal trainers or external providers.

Trainings

Training → Trainings

  1. Create a training session with date, venue/link, type, and facilitator.
  2. Invite participants — employees receive notifications in ESS → Trainings.
  3. Record attendance after the session completes.

Training Report

Training → Training Report — filter by period, type, or department to analyse participation and completion rates.
