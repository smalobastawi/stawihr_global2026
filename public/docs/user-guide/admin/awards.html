Awards

Recognize employee achievements and make awards visible in Self Service.

Award

Awards → Award

  1. Click to create a new award record.
  2. Select the employee, award title, date, and description.
  3. Save — the award appears on the employee's ESS → Awards screen and may contribute to dashboard metrics.
