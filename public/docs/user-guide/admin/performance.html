Performance Management

Run performance appraisals, supervisor/HOD evaluations, and PIP plans.

Setup

Under Performance Management → Setup:

  • Rating Scales — define scoring scales (e.g. 1–5).
  • Review Periods — open/close appraisal cycles.
  • Focus Areas — KPI categories for objectives.
  • Behavioral Items — competency criteria for behavioral scoring.

Manage Appraisals

HR launches and monitors appraisal cycles, assigns employees to review periods, and tracks completion status.

Evaluation workflow

  1. HR opens a review period and launches appraisals via Manage Appraisals.
  2. Employees complete self-assessment in ESS → My Performance.
  3. Supervisors complete Supervisor Evaluation.
  4. Heads of Department complete HOD Evaluation where configured.
  5. HR reviews final scores and closes the period.

PIP Plans

Performance Management → PIP Plans — create and monitor Performance Improvement Plans. Employees view assigned plans under ESS → My PIP Plans.

Reports

  • Department Report, Employee Report, Summary Report
  • PIP Dashboard, PIP By Department, PIP By Outcome
