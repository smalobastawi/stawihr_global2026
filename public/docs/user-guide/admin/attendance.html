Attendance

Configure work shifts, manage biometric devices, approve overtime, and run attendance reports.

Setup

Under Attendance → Setup:

  • Work Shifts — define shift start/end times and grace periods.
  • IP Whitelist — restrict web clock-in to approved IP addresses.

Operations

  • Overtime Approval — review and approve employee overtime before payroll.
  • Biometric Devices — register and sync ZKTeco or compatible attendance devices.

Reports

Under Attendance → Reports:

  • My Attendance Report — individual employee records.
  • Daily Attendance — who was present/absent on a given day.
  • Monthly Report — aggregated monthly attendance.
  • Weekly Report — week-by-week summary.
  • Raw Reports — unprocessed device log data.

Employees also see personal attendance on the Dashboard under My [Month] Attendance.
