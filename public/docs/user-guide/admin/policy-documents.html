Policy Documents

Publish HR policies and procedures for employees to access in Self Service.

Overview

The Policy Documents module in the sidebar lets HR administrators organize and publish company documents. Employees view and download them under Self Service → Policy Documents.

Document Categories

Policy Documents → Document Categories

  • Create folders/categories such as HR Policies, IT Security, Health & Safety, Finance.
  • Categories control how documents are grouped in the employee view.
  • Rename or reorder categories as your policy library grows.

Upload Documents

Policy Documents → Upload Documents

  1. Select the target category.
  2. Upload the file (PDF, Word, etc.) and enter a display title.
  3. Set visibility — all employees or restricted groups if configured.
  4. Publish. The document appears immediately in ESS for permitted users.
Version control When replacing a policy, upload the new version and archive or remove the old file to avoid confusion.
