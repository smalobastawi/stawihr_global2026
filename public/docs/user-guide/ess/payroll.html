Payroll & Loans

View payslips, understand your pay breakdown, and track personal loans.

My Payroll

  1. Navigate to Self Service → My Payroll.
  2. Select a pay period from the list or dropdown.
  3. Click View to open the payslip on screen.
  4. Use Download or Print to save a PDF copy for your records.

The payslip typically shows gross pay, earnings, statutory deductions (PAYE, NSSF, SHIF, AHL), other deductions, and net pay.

Multi-currency payslips

If your employer pays you in a currency different from the statutory payroll currency, your payslip shows two sections:

  • Statutory section — earnings and deductions in the company base currency (used for tax and compliance).
  • Payment section — the amount actually paid to your bank, the exchange rate used, and the effective date.

If you are paid entirely in the local base currency, your payslip shows a single currency as usual.

My Loans

Under Self Service → My Loans you can:

  • View active loans and outstanding balances.
  • See repayment schedules and amounts deducted each pay period.
  • Apply for a new loan if your organization allows employee-initiated applications.
  • Track application status through the approval workflow.
Payslip not visible? Payslips appear only after payroll has been processed and approved for that period. Contact payroll if a recent period is missing.
