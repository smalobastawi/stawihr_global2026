Team, Performance & More

Additional Self Service features for communication, development, and team management.

My Team

Supervisors can open Self Service → My Team to view direct reports, their contact details, and quick links to team-related actions.

My Performance & PIP Plans

  • My Performance — complete self-appraisals during review periods, view ratings, and read supervisor/HOD feedback.
  • My PIP Plans — view Performance Improvement Plans assigned to you, track milestones, and acknowledge plan items.
  • My Development Plans — create and manage Personal Development Plans (PDPs): set SMART goals, activate your plan, and record progress at the frequency set by HR (quarterly, bi-annually, or annually). See Performance Management → PDP for the full workflow.

Trainings & Awards

  • Trainings — accept or decline training invitations, view scheduled sessions, and see attendance history.
  • Awards — view recognition and awards you have received.
  • Shifts — see your assigned work shift schedule.

Notices, documents & feedback

  • Notices — read company announcements; attachments may be available for download.
  • Policy Documents — browse and download HR policies and procedures.
  • ESS Feedback — submit structured feedback to HR by category.
  • Anonymous Feedback — submit feedback without revealing your identity.

Disciplinary, recruitment & vehicle

  • Disciplinary — view disciplinary cases and actions recorded against your profile (read-only).
  • Job Posts — browse internal vacancies and apply through the ESS recruitment flow.
  • My Vehicle — view details of a company vehicle assigned to you.
