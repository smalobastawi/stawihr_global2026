Quickstart

Sign in to StawiHR and get oriented in a few minutes.

Signing in

  1. Open your organization's StawiHR URL in a web browser.
  2. Enter your username and password, then click Login.
  3. If your organization uses SSO, click Login with Google or Login with Microsoft instead.
  4. If two-factor authentication (2FA) is enabled, enter the OTP sent to your email or phone.
  5. On first login you may be prompted to change your password — follow the on-screen instructions.
Account inactive? If you see a message that your account is inactive, contact your HR or IT administrator. Only active users can access the system.

Forgot password

  1. On the login page, click Forgot Password?
  2. Enter the email address registered to your account.
  3. Check your email for a reset link and follow the instructions to set a new password.

After you sign in

You are taken to the Dashboard — your home screen with summary cards, notices, and shortcuts. The left sidebar shows every module you are permitted to use.

Tip Bookmark the User Guide link from the sidebar or login page so you can refer to it anytime while working in StawiHR.
